Good morning everyone. My name is James Barrett and I am the project manager for the new residential complex in East London. Today I would like to update you on where we stand. The project involves the construction of three apartment blocks, each with six floors, for a total of one hundred and forty four units. We are currently in the structural phase. The concrete frame for Block A is now complete, and we have just started the formwork for Block B. Unfortunately, we have experienced a two week delay due to supply chain issues with the reinforcing steel. The original completion date was set for March 2027, but we now expect to finish by mid April. On the financial side, the total budget is twelve point five million pounds. So far, we have spent approximately seven million, which is slightly above the initial forecast. The overspend is mainly due to rising material costs, particularly for cement and timber. We are currently reviewing the specifications with the architect to identify potential savings on the finishing works without compromising quality. Finally, regarding safety, we have had no major incidents on site, which is very encouraging. Thank you.
