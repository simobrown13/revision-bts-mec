Good afternoon. I am Claire Dupont, cost consultant at Henderson Associates. I will be presenting the preliminary cost estimate for the Riverside School extension project. The scope of works includes a new two storey classroom block with twelve rooms, a gymnasium, and the refurbishment of the existing reception area. Our estimate is based on current market rates and benchmarking data from three similar education projects completed in the past eighteen months. The total estimated construction cost is four point eight million pounds. Substructure and foundations represent approximately eight percent of the total. The superstructure, including the steel frame, cladding, and roofing, accounts for thirty five percent. Mechanical and electrical installations make up twenty two percent. Internal finishes represent eighteen percent. External works and landscaping account for seventeen percent. I should highlight two key risks. First, the geotechnical survey identified potential contamination, which could increase foundation costs by up to fifteen percent. Second, the current market for steel is volatile, prices have increased by twelve percent over the last quarter. We have included a contingency of seven point five percent to cover these uncertainties.
