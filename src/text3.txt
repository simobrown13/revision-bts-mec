Right, listen up everyone. Before we start work today, I need to go through the safety briefing. My name is Tom Williams, I am the health and safety officer for this site. First, a reminder about personal protective equipment. Hard hats, high visibility vests, and safety boots must be worn at all times on site. If you are working at height, you must also wear a harness. We have had two near miss incidents this week. On Monday, a scaffold board was not properly secured and fell from the third floor. Fortunately, nobody was underneath. On Wednesday, a delivery truck reversed without a banksman guiding it. Both situations could have resulted in serious injuries. From today, we are implementing new rules. All scaffolding must be inspected every morning before work begins. No vehicle movement in the loading area without a trained banksman present. Also, please remember that the tower crane will be operating in zone B today, so keep clear of that area unless you have authorisation. If you see anything unsafe, report it immediately to your site supervisor. Safety is everyone's responsibility.
